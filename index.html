Know your luthier

Nesto

With nearly a decade experience building and repairing instruments, Nesto offers the highest standard of service to each of his clients.



Ernesto (Nesto) Hernandez is a certified luthier. Repairing and building guitars for local as well as international musicians, he takes pride in the work he does on every instrument that passes through his hands. With the finest attention to detail, Nesto assures a standard of excellence in his craftsmanship from custom instruments to simple repairs. Prior to starting his own repair company, Nesto served as Master Luthier and District Manager of Raritan Bay Guitar Repair, where he was contracted to oversee operations in New England area repair departments of a national music retailer.

Nesto trained and studied at Musicians Institute Guitar Craft Academy in Hollywood, CA, the Eastern School of Fretted Instruments Repair in Freehold, NJ and the Taylor Guitars factory on UV finish techniques. He has spent the past several years serving musicians in Boston, MA.

In addition to repair and custom instrument craftsmanship, Nesto has also extended his experience to production and back line work at a number of festivals and events including the Peach Festival, Bella Terra Festival, Disc Jam Festival, and Luna Light Festival among others.
